Our Story

A Local Owned Barbecue Restaurant That Offers Something for Everyone

At Crossroads Rib Shack, we believe great barbecue brings people together. Our pitmasters rise before dawn to stoke the fires and begin the slow, patient process of smoking the finest meats to perfection. Every rack of ribs, every brisket, every pulled pork sandwich is crafted with care and seasoned with our signature house dry rub — what our regulars call “the dust.”

With two locations proudly serving North Mississippi — our original Corinth restaurant and our newer Ripley spot — we’re on a mission to deliver fall-off-the-bone flavor with genuine Southern hospitality. From our three house-made BBQ sauces to our scratch-made sides, every detail matters.
